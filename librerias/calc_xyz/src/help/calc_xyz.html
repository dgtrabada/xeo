Calc_xyz


The input:

Path_1/file_1.xyz step_1
Path_2/file_2.xyz step_2
Path_3/file_3.xyz step_3
.     .      .
.     .      .
.     .      .
Path_N/file_N.xyz step_N

All the files must have the same number of atoms.
In the example of the image: file_1=file_2="/home/dani/bin/xeo/examples/example_benceno/answer.xyz"
with step_1=1 and step_2=80


x[1]-x[2] = diference between x[file_1] step_1 and x[file_2] step_2
in this case is the diference between the step 1 and 80 of the same film("xyz")

x[1]-y[5]*8+z[2]/x[7] = x[file_1]-y[file_5]*8+z[file_2]/x[file_7], (the step which correspond)

If you press interpolate, the program interpolates between step 1 and step 80 with 5 frames (In the case of the image).
